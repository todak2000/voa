FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Customers who ship their orders within Nigeria should expect to receive their orders within five (5) to ten (10) working days upon payment verification depending on the volume of orders received.

If you experience delays in receiving your order, contact us immediately and we will help to confirm the status of your order.

On Order placement, the order tracking list would be sent to your email. Also the status of your checkout history can be found under Order History.

We take such matters very seriously and will look into individual cases thoroughly. In the event of damaged product received, we will require photo proof of the affected product and your D.O (Delivery Order) in order for us to investigate and review before a decision is made to re-send the product to you at no cost, subject to availability. In light of this, any product that falls into this category should not be thrown away before taking photo proof and emailing the photo to us at help@voa.com.

We regret to inform you that no refunds will be given for orders that does not meet the above requirements for replacement or refund. Thanks for your cooperation
